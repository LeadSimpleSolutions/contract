Website Service Agreement

Lead Simple Solutions

This Website Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Lead Simple Solutions ("Agency") and the undersigned client ("Client"). By signing this Agreement, Client agrees to the terms below regarding the creation and delivery of a lead-capture website.

1. Scope of Services

Agency agrees to provide the following services:

1. Lead Capture Website Design & Build
  • Professionally designed, conversion-focused website.
  • Clear call-to-action (CTA) placement.
  • Lead capture form above the fold.
  • Fully mobile-responsive design.
2. AI Chatbot Integration
  • AI-powered chatbot for FAQs and lead capture.
  • Configured to collect prospect information and deliver it to Client.
3. Automated Lead Follow-Up
  • Automated email/SMS "thank you" messages for users who submit forms.
  • Form submissions will be automatically sent to the Client's email.
  • Automation will also forward each new lead to the Client via text message.
4. Ongoing Support & Maintenance (Monthly Plan)
  • Website hosting and uptime management.
  • AI chatbot operation and maintenance.
  • Email/SMS automation tools.
  • Technical support and minor adjustments as needed.
2. Pricing & Payment Terms
  • One-Time Setup Fee: $1,250 - Required before work begins. Covers design, development, integrations, and launch.
  • Monthly Service Fee: $175/month - Covers hosting, AI chatbot usage, automation tools, lead notifications, and ongoing support. Begins at launch or 30 days after the setup fee is paid, whichever comes first.
  • All payments are non-refundable once work has begun.
3. Ownership & Usage Rights
  • After full payment of the setup fee, Client receives ownership of the website design, structure, and on-page content.
  • Lead Simple Solutions retains ownership and intellectual property rights to: AI chatbot setup, automation workflows, email/SMS tools and configurations, and any proprietary systems or scripts.
  • Client receives a license to use these systems while the monthly service fee remains active.
  • If monthly payments stop, hosting, chatbot, automations, and related services may be suspended.
4. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to: provide business details, content, branding, and needed access; respond promptly to avoid delays; maintain an active payment method for recurring services.

5. Revisions
